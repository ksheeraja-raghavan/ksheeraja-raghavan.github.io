Creative adventures do not always look dramatic from the outside. Sometimes they are small decisions: trying a new material, changing direction halfway through, or giving an idea more time.

## The In-Between Stage

I want this blog to celebrate those in-between stages, not just polished outcomes. The unfinished parts of a project often teach the most.

Three reminders I come back to:

1. Experiment before you judge the result
2. Let unfinished work breathe
3. Follow the version that feels alive

If you are following along, I hope these posts encourage you to stay playful, curious, and open to where your own process leads.
