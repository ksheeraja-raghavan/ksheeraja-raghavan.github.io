Art often begins before the final piece. It starts in observation, in collecting fragments of color, shape, memory, and mood that later find a way into the work.

## What Keeps Me Returning

What keeps me returning to creative practice is the freedom to ask questions without needing immediate answers. A sketch, painting, or written note can hold uncertainty in a way that feels honest.

Things I keep noticing:

- The best ideas usually arrive mid-process
- Mood can be as important as technique
- Looking closely changes how I make

The more I make, the more I see art as a conversation with myself and with the world around me.
