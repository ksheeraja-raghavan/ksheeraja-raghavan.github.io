Welcome to the very first post on my site. This space is where I want to document ideas as they take shape and share the creative moments that stay with me.

## Why I Started Writing Here

I am especially interested in the connection between making art and building a life around curiosity. Some posts will be personal reflections, while others will focus on process, inspiration, and experiments.

Here is what I want this blog to hold:

- Reflections on creative work
- Notes from in-progress projects
- Small discoveries that feel worth keeping

Thanks for reading from the beginning. I am excited to keep growing this archive over time.
